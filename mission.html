The Mission

Empowering the UK to design, build and operate satellites with unprecedented speed and affordability

Vision

To establish full vertical integration of satellite product generation in the UK, making it possible for university students and institutions to design, build and operate satellites quickly and affordably. We are building a sovereign British space capability that reduces development time and costs while maintaining world-class quality.

Our Approach

British-Built Excellence

All products designed and manufactured in the UK, supporting British engineering and innovation

Accelerated Development

Dramatically reduced time from concept to orbit through streamlined processes and modern tooling

Cost Reduction

Making satellite technology accessible through innovative design and efficient manufacturing

Educational Empowerment

Enabling university students to gain hands-on experience in real satellite missions

What Sets Us Apart

Full UK Vertical Integration

Complete sovereign capability from component design through manufacture, assembly, testing, launch and operations

Student-First Design

Products and platforms engineered to be accessible for university teams, fostering the next generation of space engineers

Speed and Affordability

Reducing barriers to space access through rapid development cycles and cost-effective solutions without compromising quality
