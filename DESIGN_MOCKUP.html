Design Principles

Visual First

Large, impactful imagery with minimal text overlay. Let visuals tell the story.

High Contrast

Pure black backgrounds with white text and strategic blue accents for maximum readability.

Generous Spacing

Abundant whitespace creates breathing room and emphasizes key content.

Smooth Animations

Subtle fade-in effects and hover states enhance user experience without distraction.

Performance Focus

Optimized CSS, lazy-loaded animations, and minimal JavaScript for fast load times.

Mobile Responsive

Fluid layouts that adapt seamlessly from mobile to desktop viewports.
